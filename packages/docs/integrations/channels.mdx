Users are messaged on a specific channel. Channels, like [email](/integrations/channels/email), and in the future mobile push, SMS, in-app messages, and more, each provide unique opportunity to drive engagement with your users.

## Available Message Channels

- [Email](/integrations/channels/email)
- [SMS](/integrations/channels/sms)
- [Webhooks](/integrations/channels/webhooks)
